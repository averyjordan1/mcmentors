Students Mentors Resources

So, you're thinking about going out of state?

You’re not alone! Graduates from Madison Central have gone on to great schools all across the country. School represented in our alumni network include nearby universities such as the University of Alabama, Louisiana State University, and the University of Oklahoma. Schools that are much further away are also represented including Duke University (North Carolina), Rice University (Texas), Marymount Manhattan College (New York), Stanford University (California), and Yale University (Connecticut). Wherever you’re trying to go, our network can give you the inside scoop on the application process and student life at the universities you’re interested in!

Get the inside scoop from students who were just in your shoes

Our mentors were just in your shoes (you probably know a lot of them!), so they can give you all the details about the application process - things like what tests to take, the best way to approach essays, and special scholarship opportunities ($$$$). Mentors can also help you get a good idea if you would like it at the school you are applying. The insights you’ll get from real students will be things that you can’t find in a college brochure.

Questions? Answers.

Applying to college is complicated, but finding answers to questions doesn't need to be. Now you can call upon the collective knowledge of past MC students to help answer all your tough college application questions.

Personalized Editing

MCM is able to offer a limited number of students one-on-one mentorship. An editing mentor is paired with one student over the course of the semester, and together the mentor and student work through the entire process of writing college essays, from first draft to clicking submit.

YOUR Universities

MCM has mentors from over 50 different universities across the country, who are here to answer all your questions. Once you join MCM you have direct access to their insider knowledge about the school you're interested in.
