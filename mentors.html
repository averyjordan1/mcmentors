Come full Circle

Madison Central High School is your alma mater. And now, you have the opportunity to give back to current students. Just a few years ago, you faced the same tough decisions they face now: what schools should I apply to? Can I really go out of state? How exactly do I go about applying to college? By adding your knowledge and experience to the collective knowledge of MCM, you can help the next generation of Madison Central students achieve what you did.

Help how you can

As a college student, you have a busy schedule. There’s no way around it. That’s why we’ve created a variety of mentorship positions to meet mentors time constraints. You can serve in different capacities, from being a University Ambassador and discussing your university’s social life and academics to being a Writing Mentor and helping edit a single student’s application essays from start to finish. When you apply to be a mentor, you can select whichever positions interest you, and we’ll let you know where we need you most.

Represent your University

By serving as a mentor you have the opportunity to represent your university to current Madison Central students. You get a chance to share the details that can’t be found in college information brochures and on college confidential. We welcome people who offer balanced opinions of their university: you’re encouraged to share both upsides and downsides that you’ve noticed in your time there.
