Students Mentors Resources

How can you say "thank you" to Madison Central?

MCM is a network of dedicated and motivated MC graduates who have chosen to attend out of state colleges and universities. If you're looking for a way to give back to the Madison Central community, this is your chance. Take a look below at how mentors are involved in the network, and what you can expect to be doing as a mentor in the network. After reading, you can join using the form at the bottom.

It's your community, too!

Madison Central depends on students to maintain such impressive achievement levels. But no one said that it's only up to current students to contribute to the community. Serving as a MCM mentor is a a way for alumni to contribute to the Madison Central community's success.

Come Full Circle

Madison Central High School is your alma mater. And now, you have the opportunity to give back to current students. By adding your knowledge and experience to the collective knowledge of MCM, you can help the next generation of Madison Central students achieve what you did.

On Your Time

As a college student, you have a busy schedule. There’s no way around it. That’s why we’ve created a variety of mentorship positions to meet mentors time constraints. When you apply to be a mentor, you can select whichever positions interest you, and we’ll let you know where we need you most.

Share Your School

By serving as a mentor you have the opportunity to introduce your university to current Madison Central students. We welcome people who offer balanced and honest opinions of their universities: you’re encouraged to share both the upsides and the downsides of your college experience.
